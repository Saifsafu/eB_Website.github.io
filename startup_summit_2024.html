Inaugural Brunei Startup Summit 2024

  • Rokiah Mahmud
  • July 20, 2024

The inaugural Brunei Startup Summit 2024, held at The Rizqun International Hotel, Gadong, gathered a diverse group of local and international startups, aspiring entrepreneurs, industry experts, investors, and stakeholders.

Organized by the Brunei Economic Development Board (BEDB) in collaboration with Darussalam Enterprise (DARe) and the Brunei Innovation Lab (BIL), the summit aimed to foster growth, collaboration, and innovation within the Sultanate’s startup ecosystem. The theme of the summit was ‘Innovate to Elevate: Charting the Path to a Future of Possibilities’.

Minister at the Prime Minister’s Office and Minister of Finance and Economy II Dato Seri Setia Dr Awang Haji Mohd Amin Liew bin Abdullah, as the Chairman of BEDB and DARe, officiated the event.

Acting Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of BEDB and DARe, Daniel Leong, expressed his gratitude to all stakeholders involved in coordinating the summit. He emphasized the event’s goal of uniting startups, ecosystem builders, and venture builders to create a collaborative environment for mutual learning, sharing insights, discussing industry trends, and engaging in dialogue sessions.

Summit Highlights

The two-day event featured local and regional speakers, panelists, and guests from various sectors, facilitating collaboration and knowledge exchange. Participants were encouraged to embrace challenges, celebrate successes, and foster an environment where collaboration is celebrated.

The summit received support from program partners, including Brunei Shell Petroleum Company Sdn Bhd, Progresif Sdn Bhd, EVYD Technology Sdn Bhd, Authority for Info-communications Technology Industry of Brunei Darussalam (AITI), and Microsoft Brunei.

Launch of Startup673

The opening ceremony marked the launch of Startup673, a collaborative initiative led by four agencies – Shell LiveWIRE Brunei, BEDB through DARe, AITI, and BIL. This initiative aims to integrate existing initiatives from these agencies into a streamlined and unified program.

Startup673 seeks to accelerate the development of groundbreaking solutions benefiting the entire entrepreneurial ecosystem. It serves as a platform where innovation meets entrepreneurship, fostering a dynamic environment for innovative ideas to evolve into thriving businesses. The program will be carried out through a three-phase approach: Ideate and Innovate; Validation and Prototyping; and Growth and Development.

The first phase, set to kick off with a hackathon in April 2024, aims to empower individuals from diverse backgrounds to address pre-defined challenges posed by industries. This phase includes workshops, both technical and non-technical, culminating in a three-day hacking session where participants collaboratively develop proofs of concept and minimum viable products before pitching to a panel of judges.

Participating in the Startup673 program provides entrepreneurs with networking opportunities within the entrepreneurship ecosystem, enabling connections with like-minded individuals, mentors, and potential collaborators.
